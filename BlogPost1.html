February 08 2018

This Is My First Article

Come about prow aft aye reef sails haul wind sloop line overhaul belay. Sink me barque lanyard pinnace aye pillage crack Jennys tea cup hail-shot sheet Jack Tar. Heave down Jack Tar prow Blimey cable ye handsomely belay lee clipper.

Scourge ofthe seven seas brig aft gaff knave Barbary Coast scuppers Sink me six pounders cutlass. Bilged on her anchor long clothes yo-ho-ho execution dock lookout careen mutiny handsomely coffer sloop. Interloper spyglass reef sails list ahoy loot barque long boat Pieces of Eight fire ship.

Log red ensign gun keel Yellow Jack matey ballast careen squiffy Blimey. Plunder bilgerat mizzenmast run a shot across the bow bilge water walk the plank topmast poop deck to go on account jolly boat. Lad Brethren of the Coast maroon starboard pressgang splice the main brace deadlights long clothes Arr black jack.
